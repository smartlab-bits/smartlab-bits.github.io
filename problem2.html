SmartLAB intends to develop a medication reminder application.
It should be a very flexible application which can handle a large variety of medicine schedules. e.g: Pill A - Day 1-3 : 3 times a day, Day 4-5: 2 times a day; Pill B - lifetime every morning 1 pill; Pill C - 1/4th pill in the morning, 3/4th in the night before food for 5 days, etc.

  1. Design the application. [You may extend/modify the idea] Provide a description of the end user experience of your application.
  2. Design an intuitive way to take user input for this application.

Note - This is a design question
